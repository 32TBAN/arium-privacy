Account Deletion - ARium

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from ARium, follow the steps below:

How to request account deletion

  • Send an email to: teban5169@gmail.com
  • Use the subject: "Eliminar cuenta"
  • Include in the email the account email you want to delete
  • You can also send the request easily from within the app in the "Help and Feedback" section
  • Requests are processed within 5 business days

Data deletion

When your account is deleted, the following data will be removed:

  • User account information
  • Associated activity and app usage data

Data retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security and legal purposes, after which it is permanently deleted.

Contact

If you need assistance, contact: teban5169@gmail.com
